Free Fundraising For Everyone

We take 0% platform or card processing fee. Get more for your money.

What Makes Us Different?


Why we stand out over other crowd funding websites is our percentage take. If you use other crowd funding sorces (ex. GoFundMe, Kickstarter, etc.) they have a percentage take. Typically it is 5% for platform fees and another 2.5% for payment processing. If you use Funder, we do not charge any platform fees and all payment processing if 100% covered by us. By doing this, more money goes into your funds in order to accomplish the goals you set out to achieve.

How it Works


Start by creating a funder. This is where you're going to make your fundraising page (what it is for, your goals, etc.)


Once the form is finished, the funder will post and people will be able to see your funder. Once people log on and see your funder, if they decide to donate, there will be a donate button associated with your funder.


Once the donation is received, the money will be added to your goal. Now this is where we're different. 0% platform fee will be taken out and the processing fee is 100% covered by us. All of the money donated will go directly to you and your fundrasing goals.
